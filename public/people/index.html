Principal Investigator#

Raghunandan M. Rao#

I received the B.Eng degree in Telecommunication Engineering from R. V. College of Engineering in 2011, the M.Tech degree in Laser Technology from IIT Kanpur in 2013, the M.S degree in Electrical Engineering from Virginia Tech in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from Virginia Tech in 2020.

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Gandhinagar. Prior to this, I held wireless systems engineering positions at Amazon Lab126 and Samsung Research America. My current research focuses on localization, Non-Terrestrial Networks (NTN), Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC), and 6G wireless networks.
