# Wireless Communications Localization and Sensing Lab
Welcome to the **Wi**reless **Co**mmunications **L**ocalization and **S**ensing **(WiCoLS)** Lab at IIT Gandhinagar!  

**Location:** *AB13/309, Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar*


## Current Research Focus
The current research focus of this lab is to advance the theory and practice of the following topics:
- 6G Non-Terrestrial Networks (NTNs)
- Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC)
- Wireless Localization
